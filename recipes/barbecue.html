Barbecue

Description

Barbecue is the wonderful dish. People from Rwanda are enjoying the mysteries that come with barbecue. Chef responsible for the preparation always have a tight schedule.

Remember to sit with your friends and enjoy wonderful times. Barbecue offers a good opportunity for friends to catch up, for families to come together, but most of all for individuals to enjoy themselves.

Ingredients

  • Meat
  • Cheese
  • Beef
  • Fruits
  • Pepper

Steps

  1. Prepare the stove
  2. Place the beef or meat on stove
  3. Once the meat is ready, add some cheese
  4. Mix with fruits accordingly
  5. Serve your family and friends
